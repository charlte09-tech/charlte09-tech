TikTok and the Attention Span of Today’s Generation

Exploring how fast-paced content shapes our focus.

Introduction

In the recent years, TikTok has become one of the most influential platforms in shaping the way young people consume content. Unlike traditional media, where television shows, articles, or even YouTube videos demand a longer span of attention, TikTok’s short-form videos encourage users to swipe quickly from one video to another. The endless feed of clips has created a social environment where rapid consumption is the norm. In this proposal, we want to understand whether such habits may have long-term effects on our ability to focus and sustain attention on demanding tasks.

Why is this topic important

Attention span does not only indicate the measure of how long someone can stay focused, it also significantly impacts learning abilities, productivity, creativity, and even emotional well-being. If our brains adapt to expecting fast-paced changes in content, it may reduce our patience in dealing with complex problems. On the other hand, short-form platforms could argue that TikTok motivates creativity by encouraging people to tell stories or convey messages in a limited time. The debate lies in whether this creative exercise outweighs the potential downsides of reduced focus.
